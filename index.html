About Me

Hello, I'm Syarifah Aisyah. I weave words into tapestries of thought, capturing the ephemeral beauty of life through poetry and prose.

With a heart attuned to the whispers of the world and a mind that seeks wisdom in everyday moments, I transform observations into reflections that resonate with the human experience.

My writing explores themes of nature, identity, love, and the quiet moments that often go unnoticed but hold profound meaning.

"Words are the vessels that carry our souls across the ocean of human experience."

My Writing

Whispers of Dawn

Morning light filters through
Gossamer curtains of dew,
Each droplet a universe
Reflecting possibilities anew.

I stand at the threshold
Between night's retreat and day's advance,
Gathering courage in handfuls,
To face the world's uncertain dance.

Solitude's Embrace

In quiet corners of my mind
I find the peace that time denied,
A sanctuary built of thought
Where wisdom waits to be untied.

The world may rush in torrents wild,
But here, in silence reconciled,
I learn the language of my soul
And hear the universe's child.
