The Wailing of the River

An Environmental Data & Poetic Awakening

About the Project

This project combines poetry with environmental data to tell the story of our rivers and their struggle against pollution and exploitation.

Through the lens of poetic expression and scientific analysis, we explore the impact of human activity on these vital waterways and the ecosystems they support.

"The river's voice speaks of both beauty and suffering - we must learn to listen."

The Wailing of the River

The unspoken creation of God,
Home to water creatures,
The essence of mankind—
It bestowed upon us a stream of life.

But what did we give in return?
We repaid it with:
Spills of poison,
Bags of plastic,
And piles of trash—
All from our hands.

This was our version of gratitude
This was our twisted reciprocity.

Now, we reap the bitter fruit—
A dying vein of the bitter Earth.
How cruel we are, right?

Could we change?
